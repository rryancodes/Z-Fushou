## What is the refund policy for Z.ai subscription plans?

**Problem description:**
Users who subscribed to GLM Pro annual plan expected increased token usage capacity but found the effective usable quota more restricted due to peak-hour multipliers (3x–5x) and weekly caps. After experiencing this mismatch between marketing expectations and actual usable capacity, users requested refunds for their annual subscriptions.

**Solution:**
**Z.ai subscription plans are non-refundable once purchased.** This policy applies even if you haven't used your full plan quota.

**Official Refund Policy:**
- Subscriptions fees cannot be returned after purchase
- This applies to all plan tiers (Lite, Pro, Max)
- This applies to all billing cycles (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Unused quota does not qualify for refund

**Before purchasing a subscription:**
1. Review the [Billing and Invoices documentation](https://docs.z.ai/devpack/overview#billing-and-invoices)
2. Choose a subscription plan that fits your usage needs
3. Select an appropriate billing cycle (monthly vs annual)
4. Understand the quota structure including:
   - Weekly caps on token usage
   - Peak-hour multipliers that increase token consumption
   - Difference between Lite, Pro, and Max plan allowances

**If you have billing issues:**
- Contact support through Discord #bad-case-report channel
- Email support with your User ID and billing details
- Note that policy exceptions are rare and handled case-by-case
